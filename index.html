Sales Tracker

Track whole-batch investment, itemized expenses, sold items, paid orders, unpaid orders, unsold stock, date range filters, and export to Excel-ready CSV.

Quick guide: 1) Choose investment mode. 2) Fill product, quantity, price, ordered, and paid. 3) If using itemized mode, add expense name and amount, then press Add Expense. 4) Press Add Record to save. 5) Use date filters if needed. Unsold quantity is total items minus ordered. Unpaid is ordered minus paid.
